How much does it cost to attend codebar Festival?

It is 100% free! We are really lucky to have some great companies who are supporting codebar Festival this year.

If you are in the UK, we may have some budget to page for your travel. Please fill in this form. Unfortunately, if you're outside the UK, we don't have budget. We would love to help everyone but securing sponsorship this year has been tougher than ever before.
